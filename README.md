##  The Problem

When disasters strike, misinformation spreads faster than truth. Lives are lost not just from the crisis itself, but from the chaos caused by false information:

- ** Real Impact:** During the 2023 Manipur violence, fake videos sparked communal riots
- ** Mumbai Floods:** False shelter locations left families stranded in rising waters
- ** The Statistics:** Misinformation spreads 6x faster than verified information during emergencies

**Current solutions fail because:**
- ‚ùå Fact-checking websites are too slow (hours/days after damage is done)
- ‚ùå Government SMS alerts are one-way and generic
- ‚ùå Social media monitoring is reactive, not proactive
- ‚ùå No coordination between authorities and citizens

---

##  Our Solution

**CrisisGuard** is a dual-platform system that fights misinformation during crisis situations through:

1. ** Early Detection:** AI agents that catch rumors at their source
2. ** Predictive Intelligence:** Forecasting which lies will spread before they go viral
3. ** Crowd Power:** Gamifying truth verification to mobilize citizens
4. ** Real-Time Response:** Giving authorities tools to respond at the speed of social media

### Two Platforms, One Mission

####  **Mobile App (Citizens)**
Empowers everyday people to access truth and fight misinformation

####  **Web Dashboard (Authorities)**
Mission control for detecting, analyzing, and neutralizing rumors at scale

###  Authority Dashboard (Web Platform)

The authority platform is architected in two powerful layers:

---

###  **DETECTION LAYER**

Catches lies at their source before they spread.

#### 1. **Digital Watermarking Studio** 
```
‚úì Embeds tamper-proof metadata in all official content
‚úì C2PA standard + blockchain verification
‚úì Instant authentication: "Is this really from the government?"
‚úì Makes forgery of official communications impossible
```

**How it works:**
- Authority uploads video/image/document
- System embeds invisible cryptographic signature
- Citizens can verify authenticity instantly
- Any tampering breaks the signature

#### 2. **Bot Network Detector** 
```
‚úì Identifies coordinated disinformation campaigns
‚úì Maps bot networks amplifying false rumors
‚úì Tracks fake account clusters and their connections
‚úì Exposes the puppet masters behind organized misinformation
```

**Detection Methods:**
- Behavioral analysis (posting patterns, timing)
- Network topology mapping
- Natural language processing for bot-like content
- Cross-platform tracking

**Stats:** ~25% of crisis-related social media activity is driven by bots

#### 3. **Deepfake & Synthetic Media Analyzer** 
```
‚úì Real-time image/video manipulation detection
‚úì Audio authenticity verification
‚úì Flags AI-generated synthetic media
‚úì Prevents fake official statements from spreading
```

**Technology Stack:**
- Computer vision models for visual artifact detection
- Audio forensics for voice synthesis detection
- Metadata analysis for source verification
- Machine learning trained on known deepfakes
